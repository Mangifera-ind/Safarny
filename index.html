Taal, volcano, Batangas

Taal Volcano, located in the province of Batangas, Philippines, is one of the most active volcanoes in the country. The volcano is known for its picturesque views and the unique feature of having a lake within the crater, which itself contains a small island. This destination offers an adventurous hike with stunning landscapes and is a must-visit for nature enthusiasts.

Brazil, Rio De janiro

Rio de Janeiro, known for its stunning beaches like Copacabana and Ipanema, is a vibrant city in Brazil. The city is famous for its carnival festival, the iconic Christ the Redeemer statue, and the Sugarloaf Mountain. Visitors can enjoy a rich cultural experience, samba music, and breathtaking natural scenery.

France, Paris

Paris, the capital city of France, is renowned for its art, fashion, and culture. The city is home to world-famous landmarks such as the Eiffel Tower, Notre-Dame Cathedral, and the Louvre Museum. Paris offers a romantic ambiance, exquisite cuisine, and a myriad of historical and cultural attractions.

Trip to Monza

Monza, a city in Italy's Lombardy region, is best known for its Grand Prix motor racing circuit, Autodromo Nazionale Monza. Beyond the roar of engines, discover the Royal Villa of Monza with its beautiful gardens and the historical Cathedral of Monza, home to the Iron Crown of Lombardy.

Trip to New Orleans

New Orleans, Louisiana, is a vibrant city known for its round-the-clock nightlife, live-music scene, and spicy, singular cuisine reflecting its history as a melting pot of French, African, and American cultures. Explore the French Quarter, listen to jazz in the street, and savor the flavors of Creole cuisine.
